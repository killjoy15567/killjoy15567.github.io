The common Dungeon Master often looks too narrowly; having bandets
that pop up somehow have a abanded prison or mine to set up shop in, or
maybe orges have taken up residence in a farmers house.
What you can do instead of that is have:

  1. Saving a prince or a wealthy noble held captive in their own castles.
  2. Kobolds hiring warriors to defeat the goblins they are at war with.
  3. An Ancient Dragon demanding human tributes from citys surrounding its lair.
